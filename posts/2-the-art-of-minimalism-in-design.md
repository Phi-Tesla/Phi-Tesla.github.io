Minimalism in design isn't about removing things until nothing is left. It's about removing distractions until only the essential remains.

## White Space is Your Friend

Also known as negative space, white space gives content room to breathe. It guides the eye and makes reading easier. Don't be afraid of empty space on a page — it's working for you.

## Typography Matters

When you strip away decorative elements, typography becomes the primary visual tool. Choose fonts that are readable, set proper hierarchy with size and weight, and limit yourself to two or three typefaces.

## Color with Purpose

A minimal palette doesn't mean boring. Pick a primary color, a neutral background, and use accent colors sparingly to draw attention to what matters most.

The goal is always the same: help the user find what they need with the least amount of friction.

设计中的极简主义不是删除到空无一物，而是去除干扰，只保留精髓。

## 留白是你的朋友

留白（也称为负空间）给内容以呼吸的空间。它引导视线，让阅读更轻松。不要害怕页面上的空白区域——它在为你服务。

## 排版至关重要

当你去掉装饰性元素后，排版就成为了主要的视觉工具。选择易读的字体，用大小和字重设置清晰的层次，限制在两到三种字体以内。

## 有目的地使用色彩

极简调色板并不意味着无聊。选一个主色，一个中性背景，少量使用强调色来吸引注意力到最重要的地方。

目标始终如一：帮助用户以最小的阻力找到他们需要的东西。
