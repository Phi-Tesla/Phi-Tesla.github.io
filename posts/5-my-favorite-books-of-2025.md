Reading is one of the best investments of time. Here are five books from 2025 that I can't stop thinking about.

## 1. "Piranesi" by Susanna Clarke

A haunting, beautiful novel about a man who lives in an endless House filled with statues and tides. The mystery unfolds slowly and the ending is devastating in the best way.

## 2. "Sapiens" by Yuval Noah Harari

A sweeping history of humankind that changes how you think about everything from money to religion to empires. Dense but readable.

## 3. "Thinking, Fast and Slow" by Daniel Kahneman

The definitive book on how our minds make decisions. Kahneman's research on cognitive biases is essential reading for anyone who wants to think more clearly.

## 4. "The Pragmatic Programmer" by David Thomas and Andrew Hunt

Not a 2025 release, but a re-read. This book is full of practical wisdom that applies far beyond programming.

## 5. "Meditations" by Marcus Aurelius

Two thousand years old and still relevant. The Roman emperor's private thoughts on duty, mortality, and self-discipline are remarkably grounding.

Happy reading, and let me know what books defined your year.

阅读是最好的时间投资。以下是 2025 年让我久久无法忘怀的五本书。

## 1. 《皮拉内西》— 苏珊娜·克拉克

一部萦绕心头的优美小说，讲述了一个生活在布满雕像和潮汐的无尽之屋中的人。谜团缓缓展开，结局以最动人的方式令人心碎。

## 2. 《人类简史》— 尤瓦尔·赫拉利

一部人类历史的宏大叙事，改变了你对从金钱到宗教到帝国的一切思考。内容丰富但可读性强。

## 3. 《思考，快与慢》— 丹尼尔·卡尼曼

关于我们的大脑如何做决策的权威之作。卡尼曼关于认知偏见的研究是任何想更清晰思考的人的必读书。

## 4. 《程序员修炼之道》— 大卫·托马斯和安德鲁·亨特

不是 2025 年的新书，但我重读了。这本书充满了超越编程本身的实用智慧。

## 5. 《沉思录》— 马可·奥勒留

两千年前写成，至今仍然适用。这位罗马皇帝对责任、死亡和自律的私人思考异常踏实。

祝阅读愉快，也欢迎分享定义你这一年的书籍。
