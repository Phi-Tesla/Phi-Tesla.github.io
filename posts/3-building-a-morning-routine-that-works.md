After months of trial and error, I finally built a morning routine that I can stick to consistently. Here's what works for me.

## Wake Up at the Same Time

Consistency is the foundation. I set my alarm for 6:30 AM every day, including weekends. It took about two weeks for my body to adjust, but now I wake up naturally.

## No Phone for the First 30 Minutes

This was the hardest change. Instead of scrolling through notifications, I spend the first half hour making coffee, stretching, and writing in a journal.

## Plan the Day

I write down three priorities for the day. Not ten, not five — just three. This forces me to identify what actually matters instead of getting lost in busywork.

The routine isn't complicated, and that's the point. Complexity is the enemy of consistency.

经过几个月的反复尝试，我终于建立了一个能坚持的晨间习惯。以下是我的经验。

## 每天同一时间起床

一致性是基础。我每天（包括周末）都把闹钟设在早上 6:30。身体大约花了两周来适应，但现在我能自然醒来了。

## 前 30 分钟不看手机

这是最难做出的改变。我不再刷通知，而是花前半小时泡咖啡、做拉伸、写日记。

## 规划一天

我写下当天的三个优先级事项。不是十个，也不是五个——就三个。这迫使我找出真正重要的事情，而不是迷失在琐事中。

这个习惯并不复杂，而这正是关键所在。复杂性是持续性的敌人。
